## Email Sender Skill

This skill allows the Antigravity agent to compose and send email reports containing patent analysis summaries, insights, and links to the dashboard.

### Usage:
The agent will use this skill to dispatch reports to team members after analysis is complete.

### Example:
"Send the patent analysis report to the junior engineers."
